A Journey That Continues

The sea is never truly the same twice.
Familiar routes change. Winds shift.
New paths appear where none existed before.
In Catch The Wind, you return to a world shaped by salt, sun, and stories carried across the water.

Your captain's not some show-off trying to get famous. They're more like that weathered sailor who just knows stuff - like they'll glance at the clouds and go 'storm's coming' or somehow always catch the perfect wind.
This is not a race. It is a voyage.
